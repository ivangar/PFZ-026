Learning Objectives

After viewing this program, you will be able to:
  • Describe the objectives, design, and main findings of a recent study on the safety of smoking-cessation therapies.
  • Evaluate the benefits and risks associated with smoking-cessation therapies.
  • Provide evidence-based recommendations to patients who aim to quit smoking.
